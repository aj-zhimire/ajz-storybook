Frequently Asked Questions

How do browser notifications work?

When you enable notifications, your browser will show a small popup whenever new content is published. You can disable this anytime in your browser settings.

Will I get too many notifications?

No! I only publish new content when there's something meaningful to share. Expect quality updates, not daily spam.

Can I unfollow anytime?

Absolutely! You can disable notifications, unsubscribe from RSS, or remove bookmarks whenever you want. No hard feelings!

What if notifications don't work?

Some browsers or devices may not support notifications. The RSS feed and bookmark options are great alternatives that work everywhere.

About This Planner

This lightweight, offline‑friendly dashboard stores your progress and notes in your browser using localStorage. You can customize tasks (edit text directly), add new items, and export your notes as JSON for safekeeping.

  • Edit checklist items: Double‑click any item text to edit. Press Enter to save.
  • Add a task: Type in the quick add box below each day (appears when expanded).
  • Data: No accounts, no tracking — everything stays on your device.
